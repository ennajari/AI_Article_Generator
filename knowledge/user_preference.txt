ARTICLE WRITING PREFERENCES

Tone: Professional yet conversational
Length: 1,500-2,000 words per article
Structure: Introduction with hook, body with clear subheadings, conclusion with call-to-action
Style: Evidence-based with data points and statistics when available
Formatting: Use bullet points for lists, include quotes from experts when relevant
Citations: Include inline citations and a reference list at the end
Images: Suggest placement for relevant images or visualizations
SEO: Optimize for relevant keywords without keyword stuffing
Audience: Knowledgeable general audience with some domain expertise
